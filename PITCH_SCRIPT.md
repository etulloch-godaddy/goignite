### Cast

| Role | Description |

## [0:00–0:15] — DATA COLD OPEN

*(One slide. Big number. Presenter center stage.)*

**PRESENTER:** We surveyed 30 aspiring founders. 87% wanted to start a business — but never did. The number one reason? *(beat)* "I don't know where to start."

*(turns toward Valentina)* Meet one of them.

*(Attention shifts to Valentina at her laptop. Slide fades.)*

---

## [0:15–0:35] — THE ITCH

*(Valentina holds a small bottle of hot sauce.)*

**VALENTINA:** This is my grandma's hot sauce. Every family dinner, every party — people beg her for the recipe. For years everyone's told me, "Valentina, you should sell this." *(sets bottle down)* And I want to. I just don't know how you go from a bottle on the kitchen table to an actual business.

**IGNITE:** *(chime)* You just did. Let's figure out what you're building.

---

## [0:35–1:00] — ONBOARDING

*(Screen: GoIgnite questionnaire — `/questionnaire`)*

**VALENTINA:** *(clicks)* "I have an idea." *(Step 1)* Food & drink. *(Step 2, types)* "I want to bottle and sell my grandmother's Oaxacan hot sauce recipe." *(Step 3)* Confusing? Legal stuff... and honestly, just knowing what to do next.

*(Steps 4–7 — comfort sliders, existing assets, goal, budget — Valentina clicks through quickly, no narration.)*

*(Screen: Dashboard loads — Food & drink, Stage Starter, XP 0, missions appear.)*

**IGNITE:** That hot sauce isn't just a recipe — it's your product. Here's where we start.

---

## [1:00–1:35] — DOMAIN TASK → CHATBOT → DOMAIN API

*(Screen: mission card — "Register your domain via GoDaddy.")*

**VALENTINA:** Okay — register a domain. *(pauses)* Wait, what even is a domain? Do I need one right now?

*(Valentina opens the GoIgnite chat.)*

**VALENTINA:** *(types)* "Do I need a domain to start selling hot sauce?"

**IGNITE:** *(live `/api/chat` response)* Yes — and sooner than you think. Your domain is your brand's home. When you pitch to a retailer or investor, `abuelasfire.com` tells them you're serious. Without it, you're just another DM. The good news: it takes five minutes and costs less than a bottle of hot sauce.

**VALENTINA:** *(laughs)* Okay, sold. Let's find one.

*(Screen: GoIgnite domain search — Valentina types "abuelasfire" — live GoDaddy Domains API fires.)*

*(Screen: domain suggestions appear — `abuelasfire.com` ✓ available, `abuelasfire.co` ✓ available, `abuelasfiresauce.com` ✓ available.)*

**VALENTINA:** *(points)* There it is. `abuelasfire.com`. *(clicks Register — GoDaddy handoff)* Done. *(beat)* That's the moment it stopped being a kitchen thing.

*(XP bar animates. Achievement toast: "Secured Your Domain." +100 XP.)*

## [1:35–1:45] — TIME SKIP

*(Screen: a single card — **"3 months later..."** — fades in and out.)*

**IGNITE:** She kept showing up. One mission a day.

## [1:45–2:10] — SOCIAL MEDIA PLAN → THRIVING

*(Screen: GoIgnite social dashboard — AI-generated 7-day content plan visible.)*

**VALENTINA:** *(narrating)* GoIgnite gave me a content plan based on my niche and stage — exactly what to post, when, and why. I just followed it.

*(Screen: swipe through — Reel ideas, caption copy, hashtag sets, best posting times.)*

**VALENTINA:** Week one I posted three times. Week four, one video hit 200,000 views. *(beat)* Abuela got tagged in 400 comments.

*(Screen: social stats panel — 18,000 followers, 200K+ views, first brand outreach logged.)*

**IGNITE:** You didn't go viral by accident. You followed a plan.

## [2:10–2:30] — BUSINESS ANALYTICS DASHBOARD

*(Screen: analytics dashboard — stage progress, XP earned, missions completed, revenue logged, achievements timeline.)*

**VALENTINA:** And every step I took showed up here. My stage, my milestones, what I've built — all in one place.

*(Screen: highlights — "Builder Stage reached," "First Dollar Earned," "Media Kit Built," revenue trend line.)*

**PRESENTER:** *(from the side)* This is the accomplishments dashboard — auto-generated from mission completions. No manual entry. Complete missions, open the dashboard, share the link.

## [2:30–3:00] — INVESTOR READY → LLC → PITCH → FUNDING

*(Screen: stage roadmap — Investor-Ready lights up. Banner: "Full business suite unlocked.")*

**IGNITE:** Valentina — you hit Investor-Ready. Time to make it official.

**VALENTINA:** *(clicks mission — "Register your LLC via GoDaddy Airo")* *(screen: GoDaddy Airo LLC flow opens)* Three minutes. Abuela's Fire LLC is a real company now. *(looks at audience)* My grandma cried.

*(Screen: pitch generation — Valentina clicks "Generate Investor Pitch.")*

**IGNITE:** Here's your deck outline.

*(Screen: structured 8-slide pitch outline populates — Cover, Problem, Solution, Market, Traction, Business Model, Financials & Ask, Next Steps — each with headline, key points, speaker notes. Valentina scrolls slowly.)*

**VALENTINA:** It knows my story. Everything I built, everything I did — it's all in here.

*(Screen: Funding Engine — `GET /api/funding?stage=investor_ready&creator_type=food` — cards populate: Techstars, Backstage Capital, Goldman Sachs 10KSB, Etsy Up & Running Grant.)*

**VALENTINA:** And here's who I'm pitching to next. *(taps Techstars card)* Matched to me. Based on my stage, my niche, my creator type.

## [3:00–3:15] — BUTTON

*(Valentina picks the bottle back up.)*

**VALENTINA:** I started with my grandma's recipe and zero idea what I was doing. Now Abuela's Fire has a domain, an LLC, 18,000 followers, and a pitch deck. *(beat)* I was the 87%. Not anymore.

**IGNITE:** *(chime)* You didn't need to know where to start. You just needed to start. 🔥

*(Presenter returns; dashboard on screen.)*

**PRESENTER:** That's GoIgnite. Eight onboarding questions. A Claude-powered advisor that meets founders where they are. Daily missions across four stages — Starter, Builder, Brand, Investor-Ready. A social content engine that turns a niche into an audience. A business dashboard that auto-builds as you complete missions.

Every GoDaddy tool surfaces exactly when the founder is ready to buy it.

**PRESENTER:** The biggest untapped market isn't people with businesses — it's the 87% who want to start and never do, because no one guides them from spark to first sale. Nobody's onboarding them, because they're not customers yet. GoIgnite catches them at that moment, builds them into real founders, and delivers the right GoDaddy product — domain, email, website, LLC — precisely when they need it. *(beat)* We turn "I don't know where to start" into day one. That's GoIgnite. 🌶️🔥
